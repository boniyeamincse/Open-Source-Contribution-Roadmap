# Step 1: Getting Started with Open Source

Welcome! You are embarking on a journey to become an open-source contributor. This step outlines the goal of this roadmap and what you will achieve by the end.

## 📖 The "A to Z" Goal
This guide is a strictly linear, step-by-step roadmap. Instead of overwhelming you with complex theory, we move directly from **Discovery** to **Contribution**. By the end of this journey, you will have submitted a real Pull Request.

## 🎯 What You Will Learn
- **The Ecosystem**: How open source works and why it matters.
- **Git Mastery**: Essential tools for collaboration.
- **Project Navigation**: How to find and understand repositories.
- **The PR Workflow**: Forking, cloning, and submitting changes.

## 🛠️ Your Mission
1.  Read each step in sequence (01 to 10).
2.  Complete the practical tasks at the end of each section.
3.  Move to the next step ONLY when you are ready.
