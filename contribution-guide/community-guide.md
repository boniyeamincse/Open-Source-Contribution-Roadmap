# Community Guide

Open source is about people. This guide explains how to participate in our community effectively and respectfully.

## 🤝 Participation
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome people from all backgrounds and experience levels.
- **Help Others**: If you see someone struggling with a concept you've mastered, offer a helping hand.
- **Sharing is Caring**: Share what you learn on social media (Twitter, LinkedIn) to bring more people into the ecosystem.

## ⚖️ Code of Conduct
We follow a simple but effective Code of Conduct:
1.  **Be kind** to others. Do not insult or put down other contributors.
2.  **Be constructive** in your criticism.
3.  **Respect the maintainers** and their decisions.

## 📞 Staying Connected
If we had a community chat, this is where links to Discord or Slack would go. For now, the best way to stay connected is via GitHub Issues and Discussions.
